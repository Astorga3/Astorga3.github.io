Meteor Weather

Site Purpose

The purpose of "Meteor Weather" is to provide weather services with a little touch of an arcade game. Now days all weather websites are preaty standard and boring looking, the inted is to make it fun and atractive for the user. The simplicity of arcade games with simple menus and simple functions are also applied in this website, because we believe "The Simpler, the Better". (meteoweath.com is an available domain)
